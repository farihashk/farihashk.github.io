Solutions Architect Notes

A collection of notes and insights on the role of a Solutions Architect.

Task 1: Responsibilities and Qualifications of a Solutions Architect

Common qualifications that I came across were:

  • SQL
  • MATLAB
  • Some sort of DBMS skills like Microsoft SQL, Azure
  • A lot of lifecycle-related work - DevOps
  • A lot of APIs
  • A lot of testing
  • Salesforce certifications
  • Almost all positions required work experience and most of them required U.S. Citizenship
  • Almost every company required familiarity with what I am assuming to be company-specific tools like SUSE, DICOM, PACS
  • Compute environments like GCP, AWS, Azure; Agile methodologies like SDLC, PDLC

My understanding of the responsibilities: A Solutions Architect ideally designs architecture for software solutions from design to deployment of systems based on business needs, analyzed by closely working with sales teams. This requires knowledge of databases, cloud computing, a business perspective, and strong communication skills.
